# All R18 Results Endpoint

ParameterTypeDescription
HallticketstringRequired: Your Hallticket Number

An extremely fast (~2.89 seconds on average for getting results of 8 (from freshman(first year) to senior(final year) semesters merging all supplimentary exams) endpoint to fetch results of all semesters.

  GET /all-r18/hallticket
1

NOTE

Sometimes due to JNTUH servers being down, this endpoint might not work. Although similar requests are cached for 3 hours (currently in the latest production environment) we request you to use this feature properly due to the fact that our backend shoots multiple asynchronous requests to univesity servers at the same time which might lead to Denial of Service.

# Usage

  1. Go here:
https://results-restapi.up.railway.app
1

  1. As the above parameters have been mentioned, form the endpoint as such:

     https://results-restapi.up.railway.app/all-r18/hallticket
    1

  2. Substitute your identification values in the above parameters and the RestAPI is triggered.
